Everything you need to know about this survey
Is this survey actually free? +
Yes — 100% free. This is a genuine user research survey. Your Tinder Gold reward code is provided as a thank-you for your time, with no purchase or payment required.
Do I have to answer every question? +
Yes. All 8 questions — including the typed ones — must be fully completed to unlock the reward code. Partial submissions do not qualify.
Why are some questions typed instead of multiple choice? +
Typed answers give us much richer insights than checkboxes. We want your real voice, not just a pre-selected option. It also ensures you're a genuine user, not a bot.
How do I redeem the code? +
Open Tinder → tap your profile photo → Settings → Manage Account → Redeem a Code. Paste your code and Gold activates instantly.
How long is the code valid? +
Reward codes are valid for 30 days from the time they are generated. Redeem promptly to avoid expiry.
